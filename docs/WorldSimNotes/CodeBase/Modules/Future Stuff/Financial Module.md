# Overview
Properties
wealth - current wealth the faction has to use on trade and development
- Resources - all of the different resources that the country has stockpiled available for use and trade
	- Raw resources
		- lumber - used to develop infrastructure (naturally restores over time, can be boosted with tree farms)
			- lumber
			- lumber rate
			- lumber restoration
		- gravel - used to develop infrastructure
			- gravel
			- gravel rate (requires crusher facilities to convert stone)
			- no restoration
		- stone - used to develop infrastructure
			- stone - the actual resource
			- stone rate - how quickly you gain stone
			- no restoration
		- oil - used in fuel for transportation
			- oil - the actual resource
			- oil rate - how quickly you can find it and how quickly you are using **it**
		- coal - can be used directly to create power plants or process ore
		- electric - stores electric energy from power plants
		- ore - used to develop higher rank infrastructure
		- nuclear - used in power plants, weapons, and research (to unlock nuclear propulsion)
		- water - used in power plants and processing methods
	- Food - a good balance of all is required to sustain population
		- water - this is drinkable water, different from natural water
		- grain
		- produce
		- dairy
		- protein
		- sugar
	- Infrastructure
		- Population
			- Houses - allows higher population in the country
		- Raw Storage - allows more storage of raw resources (takes raw resources to develop)
		- Food Storage - allows more storage of produce (takes raw resources to develop)
		- Roads - reduces transportation costs on resources (takes raw resources to develop)
		- Trade Centers
	- Transportation
	- Population
		- Builders
		- Farmers
		- Transport
		- Research
		- Military
		- Medical
